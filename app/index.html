Welcome
I am happy to announce that I will join CV:HCI Lab at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany, as a post-doctoral researcher, advised by Prof. Rainer Stiefelhagen.

I recently obtained the PhD degree in Information Sensing and Instrumentation at the College of Optical Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University (ZJU), China.
My PhD research was jointly advised by Prof. Kaiwei Wang and Prof. Jian Bai at State Key Laboratory of Modern Optical Instrumentation, ZJU, as well as Prof. Luis Miguel Bergasa at RobeSafe Research Group, University of Alcalá (UAH), Spain.

Before my PhD, I obtained the B.S. degree in Measurement Technology and Instrumentation at School of Optics and Photonics from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), advised by Prof. Weiqian Zhao and Prof. Yao Hu.
I obtained the dual degree in Economics at National School of Development from Peking University (PKU).
I did an undergraduate research internship at the Department of Precision Instrument, Tsinghua University (THU), advised by Prof. Rong Zhu.

Startup
I am Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of Hangzhou KR-VISION Technology Co., Ltd., a company dedicates to assistive technologies for the visually impaired.
I am also Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of Hangzhou SUR-IMAGE Technology Co., Ltd., a startup with the aim to develop multi-modal stereo vision sensor for autonomous driving.

Research Interests
My research is in real-time computer vision for intelligent vehicles, mobile robots, and wearable systems to aid real-world navigation of visually impaired pedestrians.
Over the past few years, I have focused on traversable area parsing, semantic segmentation, polarization imaging, panoramic annular sensing, depth estimation, and obstacle detection.
Broadly I am interested in computer vision, optical imaging, visual sensing, deep learning, and their applications for autonomous vehicles, robotics, intelligent transportation systems, and assistive technologies for people with visual impairments.
